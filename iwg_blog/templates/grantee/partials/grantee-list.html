The IWG Catalytic mHealth Grant Mechanism is a UNF–WHO/HRP collaboration funded by the Norwegian Agency for Development Cooperation (Norad), and is part of the UN Secretary General’s Every Woman Every Child initiative. This grant mechanism has served as a strategic investment for accelerating mHealth innovations that focus on strengthening health systems in developing countries in order to improve RMNCH. Between 2011 and 2015, the programme supported the scaling up of 26 mHealth projects by providing funding, technical assistance and joint learning opportunities.
